Cardio Workout

Cardio workouts increase your heart rate and improve endurance. Try the following:

  • Jumping Jacks – Full-body warm-up exercise to get your heart pumping.
  • Running – Outdoor or treadmill running for 20–30 minutes.
  • High Knees – Run in place while lifting knees as high as possible.
  • Burpees – Combine squats, jumps, and push-ups for a powerful cardio blast.
  • Mountain Climbers – Fast-paced core/cardio exercise.

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

Alternate between high effort and short rest with these exercises:

  • Jump Squats – Explosive lower-body power and cardio.
  • Burpees – Total-body fat burner.
  • Skater Jumps – Lateral movement to improve agility.
  • Plank to Push-up – Core and upper-body strength.
  • Sprint Intervals – 30 seconds sprint, 30 seconds rest, repeat.

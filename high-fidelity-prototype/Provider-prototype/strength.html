Strength Training

Build muscle and increase strength with these exercises:

  • Push-ups – Great for chest, shoulders, and triceps.
  • Squats – Strengthen your legs, glutes, and core.
  • Deadlifts – Excellent for back and hamstrings (use proper form).
  • Bicep Curls – Use dumbbells or resistance bands.
  • Plank – Core-strengthening isometric hold.

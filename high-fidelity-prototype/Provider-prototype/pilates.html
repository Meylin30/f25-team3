Pilates

Pilates focuses on core strength, flexibility, and body awareness. Try these exercises:

  • The Hundred – Classic warm-up to engage your core and increase circulation.
  • Roll-Up – Strengthen your abdominals while improving spinal flexibility.
  • Swan – Back extension exercise to strengthen the spine and glutes.
  • Leg Circles – Improve hip mobility and core control.
  • Plank with Leg Lift – Challenge your core and glutes simultaneously.
